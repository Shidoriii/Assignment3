Learn more about HATSUNE MIKU and what she has to offer!

  • Vocaloid

    Hatsune Miku is a virtual pop star and cultural icon created by the Japanese company Crypton Future Media. She is part of the Vocaloid software, a voice synthesizer program that allows users to create songs using pre-recorded vocals.

  • Merchandise

    Hatsune Miku has an extensive and diverse range of merchandise that reflects her status as a global virtual icon. Her popularity spans multiple industries, including fashion, gaming, tech, and collectibles

  • Music

    What makes Hatsune Miku unique is her fan-driven success. Musicians and creators from all over the world use her voice to produce their own music, which has led to thousands of songs across genres!
